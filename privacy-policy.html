This Privacy Policy describes how Spring Data Limited (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the mobile application Noise Proof (“the App”). This page is published on GitHub Pages for App Store transparency.

1. Summary

Noise Proof is designed as a local-first utility. Incident logs, optional audio recordings, photos, and generated reports are stored on your device unless you choose to share them using standard iOS share features (for example, Mail, Files, or AirDrop).

2. Information we do not collect

In the standard configuration of the App described here, we do not:

  • require you to create an account;
  • operate our own analytics or advertising SDKs that send your incident content to our servers;
  • sell your personal data.

If we introduce optional cloud features or analytics in a future version, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy labels before release.

3. Data on your device

The App may process:

  • Incident details you enter (for example, time, tags, notes);
  • Microphone input for approximate sound level display and optional short recordings you initiate;
  • Photos you attach from your photo library or camera, if you grant permission.

This data remains under your control on your device until you export or delete it.

4. Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details. Apple may provide us limited transaction information as required to validate entitlements.

5. Legal bases (UK / EEA users)

Where UK GDPR or GDPR applies, we rely on performance of a contract (providing the App and purchases you request) and legitimate interests in operating and improving the App, subject to your rights. Sensitive permissions (microphone, photos) are used only when you use related features.

6. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your choices

  • You can revoke microphone or photo access in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security.
  • You can delete incidents and attachments within the App.
  • You can manage subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings.

8. International transfers

If you contact us by email, your message may be processed in the United Kingdom or other locations where we operate. We use appropriate safeguards where required by law.
